## 📖 What is NotchWise-Public?

NotchWise-Public is a simple tool that helps you review flashcards while working on your MacBook. It uses spaced repetition to improve memory. The app sits in your MacBook notch and shows cards without interrupting your workflow. You can study without switching windows or apps.

This tool works on macOS devices with a notch, like newer MacBook Air and MacBook Pro models.

---

## 🖥️ System Requirements

- MacBook with a notch (MacBook Air 2021 or newer, MacBook Pro 2021 or newer)
- macOS 12.0 (Monterey) or later
- At least 100 MB of free disk space

1. **Download the `.dmg` file** from the release page.

## 📚 About the Spaced Repetition Method

Spaced repetition is a way to improve memory by reviewing flashcards repeatedly over increasing time intervals. This method helps transfer knowledge from short-term to long-term memory. NotchWise automates this by scheduling cards based on your answers.
